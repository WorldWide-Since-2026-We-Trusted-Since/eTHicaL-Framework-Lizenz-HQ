================================================================================

The following axioms form the unalterable core ("unumstößliches Fundament").
No Implementation License, contract or downstream agreement may override, waive
or narrow them.

  A1. FREEDOM OF INFORMATION / INFORMATIONSFREIHEIT
      The complete corresponding source of every Covered Work, together with its
      SPDX and REUSE metadata, must remain publicly retrievable. Transparency is
      a precondition of use, not an afterthought.

  A2. PEACE OBLIGATION / FRIEDENSPFLICHT
      A Covered Work must not be used to develop, operate or materially
      facilitate weapons, autonomous lethal systems, mass surveillance in breach
      of fundamental rights, or any application whose primary purpose is to harm
      human beings.

  A3. ETHICS MATRIX / ETHIK-MATRIX
      Use must be consistent with the dignity, charity ("Nächstenliebe") and
      hope of those affected by the Covered Work. Discrimination prohibited by
      the EU Charter of Fundamental Rights is not permitted.

  A4. DSA CONFORMITY / DSA-KONFORMITÄT
      Where a Covered Work is operated as an intermediary or platform service in
      the scope of Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 (Digital Services Act), You must
      provide a working Notice-and-Action mechanism and a point of contact, and
      You must not frustrate the rights granted to recipients of the service.
