This is a NON-AUTHORITATIVE reference placeholder, created for the UECF Proof of
Concept so that data sourced from Packet Clearing House (PCH) can be expressed
with a stable SPDX `LicenseRef-` identifier. It is NOT the license itself and is
NOT legal advice.

Packet Clearing House (PCH) operates route servers at Internet Exchange Points
and publishes a global IXP directory. PCH does not publish a single, standard
SPDX open-source license for the directory data. Pending written confirmation
from PCH, downstream use under the UECF assumes at minimum:

  * attribution to Packet Clearing House as the data source;
  * use consistent with PCH's published terms and any applicable AUP.

The authoritative, current terms are published at:

    https://www.pch.net/
    https://www.pch.net/about/terms

Before any production or redistribution use, confirm the applicable terms
directly with PCH.
