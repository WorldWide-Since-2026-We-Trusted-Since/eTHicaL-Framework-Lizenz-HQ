LicenseRef-CAIDA-AUP

This is a NON-AUTHORITATIVE reference placeholder, created for the UECF Proof of
Concept so that datasets sourced from CAIDA can be expressed with a stable SPDX
`LicenseRef-` identifier. It is NOT the license itself and is NOT legal advice.

CAIDA (Center for Applied Internet Data Analysis) data is generally governed by
the CAIDA Acceptable Use Policy (AUP), not by a standard open-source license.
Typical obligations include:

  * accepting the AUP before access to restricted datasets;
  * acknowledging CAIDA as the data source in publications;
  * citing the specific dataset and its DOI where provided;
  * not re-identifying individuals and not redistributing restricted data.

The authoritative, current terms are published at:

    https://www.caida.org/about/legal/aup/
    https://catalog.caida.org/

Before any production or redistribution use, obtain and follow the official
CAIDA AUP for the specific dataset in question.
